User retention correlates strongly with onboarding completion speed under 2 minutes.

Confidence

94% verified

Sources

Author

Product Team

Last Updated

Oct 24, 2023

Data analysis across 50,000 recently acquired users indicates a statistically significant drop-off in day-30 retention for users whose initial onboarding session exceeds 120 seconds. Users completing the core setup flow in under 90 seconds showed a 24% higher LTV (Lifetime Value).

This finding contradicts earlier assumptions that "thorough" onboarding leads to better feature adoption. Instead, friction reduction appears to be the primary driver of early habit formation.
